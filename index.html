MY DESIGN PROCESS

FROM IDEAS TO ACTION

01

Understand

I start by defining the problem, scope and target audience. I conduct user research, develop personas, analyze the competition and formulate key insights before moving forward.

02

Ideate

Next, I develop user stories and flows, before sketching, building wireframes and testing my ideas through rapid prototyping.

03

Adjust

Next, I test the usability of my design and iterate upon my work to ensure a seamless user experience.

04

Deploy

Lastly, I build interactive prototypes and deliver finished assets to developers.

Working with Laura is an engaging, truly collaborative experience. She brings fresh, innovative ideas throughout the design process and seeks input from the team to promote a communal effort. Either as part of a team or working on her own, Laura is deft at ensuring projects are completed on schedule, as well as within the constraints and resources allotted without sacrificing quality. Laura is also a talented photographer and is able to skillfully incorporate her work into a design. She is always up for a challenge!
