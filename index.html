MY DESIGN PROCESS

FROM IDEAS TO ACTION

I start by defining the problem and scope of work. Then gather user research and analyze the competition.

I develop user stories and user flows, begin sketching wireframes, and conducting early testing.

Next, I refine wireframes based on testing data and develop high-fidelity mockups for further testing.

Lastly, I develop functional prototypes and deliver finished products through frontend development.
